Ceremony & Reception

Both the ceremony and reception will take place at House on the Hill, which is in the Westlake Hills area of Austin.

House on the Hill
319 Addie Roy Rd.
Austin, TX 78746

The ceremony will start at 6pm, immediately followed by a cocktail hour (yay!), then the reception (dinner and dancing).

Transportation

We highly recommend that you take a taxi to the House on the Hill. We want everyone to have a very fun and safe time, so if you think you’ll have a few cocktails, please don’t drive. Shuttle service will provide everyone a ride back. At 10pm, the shuttle will begin making runs to two locations:

  • Location 1 – Austin Motel (this will serve guests staying at the Austin Motel and San Jose Hotel)
  • Location 2 – Wyndham Garden Hotel

The last shuttle run will leave around 2am. If there are any stragglers left after that (we’ll be impressed!) we can get some taxis out there.

Attire

This is mainly for the benefit of the ladies. The ceremony and reception will be outdoors, with a certain portion on grass. So, plan your choice of heels accordingly (to avoid sinking!). We also recommend bringing a wrap/pashmina just in case it gets a little chilly in the evening. It’s usually pleasant that time of year, but just in case.
